Battle Of Bands

A rock band is a mysterious thing. Somehow, every once in a while, few individuals bump into one another. They look exactly right together, share a focus, an aspiration and the right balance of musical similarities & differences. So, do the tunes of your instruments and voice of vocalist complete each other to mould a band for which, other than rock, nothing else matters? Well! The battle of bands is just the competition for your band. Give the audience a breath-taking performance that leaves them with no other choice but to lose themselves and immerse in rock music that they will remember for a lifetime.
